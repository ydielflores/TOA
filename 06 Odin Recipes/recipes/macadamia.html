Nut Cookies

Description

I was served white chocolate macadamia nut cookies at a restaurant in Springfield, MO, during a business conference. Went home and made up this recipe which has been a hit at any church gathering I take them to. They're even better than the one I had at the conference.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • .75 cup packed light brown sugar
  • .5 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • .5 teaspoon almond extract
  • 2.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup coarsly chopped macadamia nuts
  • 1 cup coarsly chopped white chocolate

Steps:

  1. Step 1:
    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step 2:
    3. Cream butter, brown sugar, and white sugar together in a large bowl until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla and almond extracts.
  3. Step 3:
    4. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in another bowl. Gradually stir the dry ingredients into the butter mixture. Fold in macadamia nuts and white chocolate.
  4. Step 4:
    5. Drop dough by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  5. Step 5:
    6. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 10 minutes.
